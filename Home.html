Top 3 Best Rice Products in the Philippines

Sinandomeng Rice

A well-milled, soft-textured white rice with a mild flavor. It's the go-to rice for daily Filipino meals, offering excellent value for money.

Everyday meals like fried rice, adobo, and sinigang.

Dinorado Rice

Aromatic and slightly sticky rice, known for its pleasant fragrance when cooked. It's often used for special occasions and festive dishes in the Philippines.

Special meals like biryani, fiesta dishes, and family gatherings.

Jasmine Rice

A fragrant long-grain rice, known for its delicate floral aroma and soft texture. It's often used in Southeast Asian cuisines but is also popular in Filipino kitchens.

Asian dishes, stir-fries, and as a side dish for curries.
